About - Júlio Antunes

My name is Júlio Antunes, and I hold a degree in Multimedia. Currently, I reside in Lisbon, where I work for KILT Talent Agency, an audiovisual production company. Our projects span humorous content, advertising, and cinema. In my role as the Audiovisual Production Coordinator, I manage the distribution of editing and filming tasks, ensuring that every project runs smoothly from start to finish.

My responsibilities include the coordination, supervision, and management of editing teams and projects. I ensure that every team member is well-supported and that our projects are delivered on time and to the highest standard. Additionally, I handle negotiations with brands to secure audiovisual projects and partnerships, fostering relationships that enhance our creative output.

With a foundation in Multimedia, I have honed my skills in diverse areas, reflecting my passion for the fusion of technology and creativity. My work is a testament to my dedication and expertise in the field of audiovisual and multimedia production.

As you navigate through my portfolio, feel free to reach out with any questions or collaboration ideas. I'm always open to new opportunities and discussions.
