2D/3D Animation

INSIDE OTTIMA 38 PLUS

Synopsis

This project was developed in an academic context in partnership with ECOSTEEL, a company that manufactures window frames and glass. The aim of this animation is to promote their product, the OTTIMA 38 PLUS, which is a top-quality window system. The animation showcases the features of the product using both 2D and 3D animation, creating an engaging and visually appealing presentation.

The OTTIMA 38 PLUS is a durable and reliable window system that is designed to provide superior thermal insulation and noise reduction. Its sleek and modern design ensures that it will complement any architectural style.

The project progressed remarkably smoothly, primarily due to the group's exceptional organizational skills and their unwavering commitment to the project's central objectives. The team's cohesion and dedication ensured that every aspect of the project aligned seamlessly with the primary goal.

The animation proved highly successful in achieving its primary objective of effectively promoting the OTTIMA 38 PLUS. The company was not only pleased with the result but also recognized the animation as their best choice for product promotion. This acknowledgment was not only a validation of the team's hard work but also a testament to the animation's impact and quality.

Throughout the project, the team acquired valuable knowledge in the realm of company communication and client development. These newfound insights into effective communication and collaboration with a client not only enhanced the team's skill set but also shaped their professional identity, positioning them for future success in similar endeavors.
