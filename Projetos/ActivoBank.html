Digital Campaign

ActivoBank

Synopsis

This project involved the production and management of the ActivoBank Digital Campaign, where I worked as an Assistant Director and Audiovisual Coordinator. My responsibilities included supporting the director in all phases of production, coordinating the audiovisual team, and ensuring that the creative vision was executed efficiently and effectively.

I was involved in planning, organizing, and supervising the filming sessions, as well as managing communication between the creative, technical, and client teams. The campaign aimed to strengthen ActivoBank's digital presence through innovative and engaging content, always maintaining a modern and dynamic brand identity.
