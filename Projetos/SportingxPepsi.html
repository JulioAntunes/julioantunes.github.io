Podcast Recording and Editing

PEPSI X Sporting Clube de Portugal - Mete Gelo

Synopsis

I accepted the challenge of recording and editing three podcast episodes for PEPSI, in partnership with Sporting Clube de Portugal. Hosted by Guilherme Geirinhas, each episode features interviews with a player from the men's first football team and a player from the women's first football team.

The recording took place at Sporting Clube de Portugal's Estádio José Alvalade, using three Blackmagic Design Pocket Cinema Camera 4K cameras. I was responsible for directing and operating the cameras to capture three different angles. (Figure 1)
