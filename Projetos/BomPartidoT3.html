Podcast Recording and Photography

BOM PARTIDO S3

Synopsis

In this third season of Bom Partido, I worked as the audiovisual coordinator, ensuring all conditions were met for both the recording phase and post-production.

I was responsible for coordinating the entire technical side of the project, including camera setups and on-site logistics. During post-production, I managed the delivery of all audiovisual materials to the project’s key partners: SIC Notícias, Fundação Francisco Manuel dos Santos, Expresso and, most importantly, Guilherme Geirinhas, the creator and main host of the series, represented by KILT.

This season was broadcast on SIC Notícias, where it led the prime time slot, becoming the most-watched channel among news networks during its airing. The project was also distributed in podcast format by Expresso, featured on the digital platforms of Fundação Francisco Manuel dos Santos, and published on Guilherme Geirinhas YouTube channel, which served as the main hub for the series.

The season featured interviews with seven representatives from different political parties ahead of Portugal’s 2025 legislative elections.

In addition to my coordination role, I also operated three Sony FX3 cameras and a vintage Sony VHS camcorder, used for specific visual segments.
