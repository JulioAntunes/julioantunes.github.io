Podcast

KILT ROOM

Synopsis

KILT ROOM is an adaptation of an annual show previously produced by the company KILT. I was responsible for reimagining the format into a monthly podcast, each episode featuring three guests or comedians from within the company. The entire concept, production direction, and episode direction were led by me, making this project essentially my own production.

I also developed the visual identity, focusing on a clean, minimalistic, and monochromatic aesthetic—mainly black and white—to align with the company's branding and visual language. My role included overseeing the production design, directing the episodes, and supporting the overall production process.

First, 3D renders of the space were created to help understand the limitations of the location and to organize the necessary elements. The final result closely matched the intended objective, making the planning and execution of the project much easier.
