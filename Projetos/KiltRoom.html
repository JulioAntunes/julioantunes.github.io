KILT ROOM

Synopsis

I had the opportunity to work on the "Bom Partido" program, which features interviews with the top candidates for the 2024 European elections. In this project, I operated three Blackmagic Design Pocket Cinema Camera 4K units and a handheld VHS Sony DCR-DVD201E DVD Camcorder, while also taking photographs with my Canon 750D camera equipped with a Canon EFS 18-55mm lens.

I was also responsible for directing the episodes, including helping with shot composition, setting up the scene, and coordinating the lighting. The project was recorded with a visual aesthetic inspired by the 1990s and early 2000s, reflecting the past contexts of the politicians during their childhood and adolescence, and matching the architecture of the party headquarters.
