Podcast Recording and Photography

CONTEÚDO DO BATAGUAS

Synopsis

"Conteúdo do Bataguas" is a Portuguese podcast and YouTube show hosted by Guilherme Geirinhas, known for its humorous and insightful interviews with prominent figures from Portuguese culture, sports, and politics. The program stands out for its relaxed and informal tone, often blending comedy with in-depth conversation, and has become a reference in the national podcast scene.

In this project, I was responsible for the audiovisual recording and photography of several episodes. My tasks included operating multiple cameras, managing lighting setups, and capturing behind-the-scenes moments. I also contributed to the visual style of the show, ensuring a consistent and engaging look that matches the unique identity of "Conteúdo do Bataguas".

The show is recorded in a studio environment designed to create a comfortable atmosphere for guests, allowing for genuine and spontaneous conversations. My role also involved post-production support, such as editing video highlights and preparing photographic content for social media promotion.
